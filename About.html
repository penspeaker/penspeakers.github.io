PEN SPEAKER ASSOCIATION OF NIGERIA (PSAN)

Pen Speaker A\ssociation of Nigeria is a platform basically created for writers and writers in practice. A non-religious,non-ethical association,a platform where monotonous actions are expressed with pen.It is also an island where our deep rooted differences in ideologies shall be discussed without rancor. Members of PSAN are activitists who with their pen change, save and impact lives positively.

We the entire members of the PEN SPEAKERS ASSOCIATION OF NIGERIA(PSAN); A platform to illuminate minds with pen having firmly and solemnly resolved to become an association for the positive change of our society, by spreading our tentacles all through the country and strengthen our hold to achieve our numerous aims.

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

  • To organize programs that will improve the use of semantics of our members
  • To build the geomancy of our environment with our scepter - OUR PEN, for the speaking of righteousness amidst all.
  • To salvage a befitting future and also make our world well-constructed with grammatical eloquence.
  • To use our pen in changing our political atmosphere
  • To have inter-university contests in the different aspects of writing.
  • Also to come along together with writers and writers in practice in writing of articles,poems etc.
  • To make publications of our writings, news updates into our blog, magazines and the dailies.
  • To have members across states in Nigeria and African Countries with well-established broad branches and structures that will provide avenue for mobilization of writers to change our world positively.
  • To be number1 media outlet in University of Ilorin thereby, being active with our media activities

WHAT WE DO IN PSAN

  • Journalism
  • Article Writing
  • Poetry
  • Oratory Contest
  • Essay contest
  • Public Speaking

RULES AND REGULATIONS OF PSANites
