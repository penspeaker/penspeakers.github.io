DEATH

Once in a lifetime visitor
If money could win you, millionaires would live forever
If houses could appease you,Mansions would be at your beck
If cries could silence you,Tears would have your anger
You recognise no class;Rich or poor,pretty or ugly,Tall or dwarf
You respect no one and no one respects you
